Technical debt doesn’t remain merely an internal issue within the codebase.

Ultimately, it affects the deployment workflow, the CMS editing experience, component reusability, and even the consistency of the user interface.

Even in static blogs, this issue manifests itself in the form of build speed and information architecture.
